Four Fun Facts About Ghostbusters:

  • Dan Aykroyd used his family history as inspiration
  • The Marshmallow Man was actually shaving cream
  • Was going to originally be called Ghost Smashers
  • The ghost Slimer was made in memory of John Belushi

Four Fun Facts About Beetlejuice:

  • The movie is named after the star Betelgeuse
  • Sammy Davis Jr. was Tim Burton's first choice to play Beetlejuice
  • Warner Bros. wanted to change the name of the movie to House Ghost
  • Beetlejuice is only in 17.5 minutes of the 92 minute movie

Fun Facts About Hocus Pocus:

  • The movie was almost called Halloween House
  • Real moths fly out of the mouth of the actor who played the dead guy Billy
  • Leonardo DiCaprio was offered the role of Max but turned it down
  • Apparently there will be sequel premiering on Disney+ that is in the works
